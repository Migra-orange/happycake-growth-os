# $40k/month Growth Plan

## Revenue physics

HappyCake grows by increasing **local intent captured**, not by posting more random content.

Target mix:

- Existing walk-ins + loyal base: **$18k/month**
- Google Maps / local search / same-day landing page: **$7k/month**
- WhatsApp + Instagram assisted order intent: **$5k/month**
- Office / school / church recurring coordinators: **$7k/month**
- Review / referral / reminder loop: **$3k/month**

Total: **$40k/month**

## Core funnels

### 1. On the Way Home Cakes

For Sugar Land customers who decide while driving after work/school.

- Google Business post: today’s ready-made classics
- landing page: same-day pickup framing
- assistant: asks pickup time + occasion + headcount
- source-of-truth check before promising availability
- kitchen/POS handoff

### 2. Friday Office Dessert Drop

For offices, schools, churches, clinics, realtors, and coordinators.

- weekly local campaign
- Instagram/WhatsApp lead capture
- owner-approved coordinator follow-up
- recurring monthly ordering target

### 3. Box QR Loop

Every pickup becomes retention.

- happy customer → Google review
- QR saves occasion/reminder with consent
- referral: send a small sweet note to a friend
- repeat prompt before next birthday/holiday

## $500 marketing budget

- $220: Google Business/Search + IG Stories for same-day/on-the-way-home intent
- $120: Friday Office Dessert Drop tests
- $60: QR/review/reminder material
- $100: calendar moments — Eid, graduation, Mother’s Day, back-to-school, Thanksgiving

## Owner daily Telegram digest

Every morning:

1. today’s local demand angle;
2. campaign/post drafts needing approval;
3. unanswered leads;
4. kitchen capacity risk;
5. yesterday’s evidence and revenue estimate.
