# HappyCake $40k Growth Playbook

## The real constraint

HappyCake does not have a traffic problem only. It has a local-demand system problem.

The business must be present when Sugar Land customers decide:

- on the way home;
- before a family birthday;
- when an office needs dessert;
- around school/church/community moments;
- after a cake box reaches a happy customer.

## $500/month split

- $220 Google local search + Maps posts: same-day and birthday keywords.
- $120 Instagram boosts: best-performing local cake/occasion posts.
- $100 office/school/church coordinator campaign.
- $60 printed QR cards / box inserts for reviews, reminders, referrals.

## Campaigns

### On the Way Home Cakes

CTA: “Need dessert on the way home? Check today’s bake.”

Target: +5 orders/day × 26 days × $55 = ~$7,150/month.

### Friday Office Dessert Drop

CTA: “A warm dessert tray for the office before the weekend.”

Target: 20 recurring coordinator accounts × $350/month = ~$7,000/month.

### Box QR Reminder Loop

CTA: “Never forget a cake-worthy date again.”

Target: reviews + birthday reminders + referrals = ~$3,000/month after ramp.

### Calendar Moment Tables

Moments: Mother’s Day, Eid, graduation, back-to-school, Thanksgiving, Christmas.

Target: holiday preorder spikes and list growth.
